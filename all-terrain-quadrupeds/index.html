All-Terrain Quadrupeds: A Unified Policy for Locomotion, Fall & Push Recovery with Latent Estimation

International Conference on Robotics and Artificial Intelligence (ICRAI 2025)

Ömer Demirayak, Yunus Emre Akar, Hakan Temeltaş

Abstract

We present a single proprioception‑only RL policy that unifies running, stair traversal, uneven‑terrain walking, and robust fall/push recovery without mode switching. A short history encoder learns a latent context capturing slope, friction, step height, and external impulses. Trained in large‑scale simulation with extensive domain randomization and deployed zero‑shot on Unitree A1.
